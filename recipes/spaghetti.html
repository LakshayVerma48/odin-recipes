Spaghetti

Description

No two aglio e olio recipes are alike, but this one is pretty true to the classic method. The key is slowly toasting the garlic slices to a perfect golden brown in the olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti.
  2. Toast the garlic in olive oil.
  3. Stir the seasonings into the pasta, then stir in the garlic and oil.
  4. Toss with cheese.
